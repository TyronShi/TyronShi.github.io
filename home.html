Tyron Shi is a visual and motion designer from New York. He graduated in 2022 from Parsons School of Design with a BFA in Product Design and a minor in Graphic Design. His personal work draws on his interests in music, photography, and cinematography. He brings this multidisciplinary mindset to his client work, approaching every project with an openness for pushing boundaries and finding unique solutions to visual and technical challenges.
