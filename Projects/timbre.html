TIMBRE — Music as Lighting

A music reactive lamp that uses the audio qualities of a space to create a multisensory experience. TIMBRE receives music from its surrounding environment and translates the sound waves’ amplitude into numerical values to modulate the lamp’s hue and luminance levels in real time.


CAD design in Autodesk Fusion 360. Code written in Python with MU Editor. Product photography rendered with Blender 3D & design prototypes.
